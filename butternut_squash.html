Butternut Squash

Sweet, moist, and pleasantly nutty. The darker the squash, the more nutrients it contains.

Buying

Look for the ones that have a hard rind and feel heavy for their size. The stem and rind should be undamaged

Storing

The ideal storage place would be cool pantry or cabinet, one that is not as cold as a refrigerator but not as warm as near the furnace.
