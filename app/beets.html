Beets

Buying

Select small to medium beets with firm, smooth skin and no soft spots or punctures. Those with stems and leaves still attached are best. The foliage should be green and fresh-looking.

Storing

Store beets, greens and all, in a produce bag in the coldest part of your refrigerator for up to two weeks. If you don’t have room for all that foliage, snip it off about an inch above the root, but save the greens. They’re tasty, too.
