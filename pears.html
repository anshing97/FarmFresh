Pears

A truely seasonal fruit, flavors range from spicy, juicy, to flora.

Buying

Look for firm ones without bruises or spots. Pears are ripe when the skin near the stem yields to gentle pressure.

Storing

Store at room temp until ripe for up to 1 week. To speed ripening, place them in a paper bag. Ripe pears can be refridgerated for up to 3 days.
