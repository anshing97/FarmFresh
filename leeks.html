Leeks

Has a mild onion taste with a slightly sweeter edger and looks like over-grown scallions.

Buying

Look for slender, straight leeks, as those that are wider tend to have a tougher core. Avoid ones with wilted or yellowing tops.

Storing

Store leeks loosely wrapped in plastic in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.
